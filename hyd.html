Hyderabad

Hyderabad, the capital city of Telangana state in India, is a fascinating destination that showcases a blend of rich history, vibrant culture, and architectural wonders. The iconic Charminar and Golconda Fort stand as testaments to the city's architectural brilliance. With its bustling bazaars, delectable cuisine, and a booming IT industry, Hyderabad seamlessly integrates tradition with modernity, earning the moniker "Cyberabad." The city is also a hub of education and research, housing prestigious institutions like the Indian School of Business and the University of Hyderabad.
